Get Started

Worried if shipping will burn a hole in your budget?

Calculate your shipping costs for Cityswift services using our courier rates calculator. Our free calculator helps you calculate the prices for shipping items based on their volumetric weight, COD availability, dimensions, and the distance between the pick-up and delivery locations.

How Does It Work

  • Enter the 6 digit PIN code or Address of the pick-up location in the Pick-up Area Pincode field.
  • Enter the 6 digit PIN code or Address of the delivery location in the Delivery Area Pincode field.
  • Enter the approximate weight (in Kgs) in the Approximate Weight field.
    (For example, if your package weighs 1 kg, enter 1. Similarly, if your package weighs 500 gms or 300 gms, enter 0.500 or 0.300 respectively.)
  • Press Calculate to get the shipping rates and details of the different courier plans.
